Storage Room: Aisle One, Right Side

The photos begin with the left side of the top-most shelf, down against the far wall. In other words, I walked down the right aisle and begin the photos back there. I worked from left to right (toward the middle of the room). After I took pictures of the top shelf I returned to the left side of the next-lowest shelf and again worked from left to right. I finished when I reached the right-hand end of the bottom shelf.
