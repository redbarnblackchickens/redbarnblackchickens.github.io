Upcoming Events: The Afton/St. Croix Valley Policing Series

A Local Look at Policing and Mental Health

Against the background of a national and statewide focus on policing, we look at policing in our community. Our series begins with conversations about policing and mental health policy and practices.

  • Crisis Intervention Training for Cops

    Afton Indivisible member Lucia Wroblewski served over 28 years as a St. Paul police officer and has a passion for good policing. She'll give us an inside look at the training she delivers to St. Paul police about deescalating mental health crises.

    Thursday, June 3, at 6:30 pm
