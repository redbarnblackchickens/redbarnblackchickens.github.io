Afton/St. Croix Valley Policing Series

Against the backdrop of a national focus on policing, Afton Indivisible takes a local look at policing and mental health in our community.

Here's what we've been up to.

A conversation with Washington County Sheriff Dan Starry

What is the police response to mental health crises in our community? What are local policing priorities? At our July 2021 meeting we spoke with Washington County Sheriff Dan Starry about crisis intervention training, opioid reentry programs, and more in a look at policing in our community.

Crisis Intervention Training for Cops

Afton Indivisible member Lucia Wroblewski served over 28 years as a St. Paul police officer and has a passion for good policing. At our June 2021 meeting she presented an inside look at coaching St. Paul police to deescalating mental health crises.
