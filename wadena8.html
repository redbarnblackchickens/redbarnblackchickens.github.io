Section 33, Wadena County

In 1890, at age 22, my great-grandmother Emilia Vahvasenpaa Anttila Mattila walked for about four or five hours from Wadena, Minnesota, Sebeka, Minnesota, to file a homestead claim. She waded in the river to make the journey.

One hundred thirty-one years later her grandchildren post stories online about the ills of giving free things to immigrants.
